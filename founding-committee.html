Founding Committee

The founding committee of Bani Sangha Alwal was established in 1990 with the vision of promoting cultural heritage and community spirit through the celebration of Durga Puja. Our dedicated members work tirelessly to ensure that every aspect of the festival reflects our traditions and values.

Over the years, the committee has grown, welcoming new members who share our passion for preserving the essence of Durga Puja. We invite everyone to join us in this celebration of faith, culture, and community.
